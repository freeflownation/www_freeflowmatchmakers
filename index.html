Who are Matchmakers & what they do

Matchmakers feel, breathe and live by honourable values and act for the better of all. They are aware of the world’s challenges that need immediate solutions, and are inspired to fulfil the needs to bring action by enabling significant partnerships and synergistic alliances to happen.

Matchmakers are driven to connect their network of influencers and investors who desire to be agents of positive change. They bridge resources to conscious technologies and impact projects that channel more balance, harmony and health - with a view to shape a prosperous global society .

why the world needs us

Business is no longer about competitive agendas and disruptive demonstrations, that was lifetimes ago. We've come to an era where taking a stand against the status quo through collaboration is only the way forward. The time is now and we will embrace holistic approaches to power unified solutions that serve the highest good for our planet and all life on it. Matchmakers bridge the gaps between creators, producers, investors and countries with this new and collaborative mindset.

For Countries

Local, National and Global leaders have realised the upmost significance of their roles for this upcoming decade. Humanity is at a stage of exponential evolution and as a global society, we are about to witness more change than ever before.

Integral, loyal, and honest communication is key to spread awareness on the areas to be addressed for positive evolution. Actions speak louder than words, so what we say we must do.

We hold the responsibility to provide conscious technologies and solutions to protect the future of humanity and our planet. With equality at the core of the collective approach, awareness will be spread, adoption facilitated, and evolution enabled, for the highest good of all.

Matchmakers help countries connect with those conscious creators and bridges to implement them within their geographical areas. Not only will we help countries adopt the best solutions, but we will share all the knowledge and tools required for them to be independent.

For Investors

Entrepreneurs and inventors of the conscious collective have already developed the necessary technologies and solutions to bring the evolution we all believe is as the new paradigm. It’s time to act now, not tomorrow.

Impact influencers and investors are the catalysts to manifest the creation, consolidation, and sharing of these conscious technologies. The importance to drive our resources to the right projects are of upmost importance today to bring the necessary actions in time.

It’s not about the money anymore, it’s about the impact. The collective approach will enable fast connections, effective resource integrations, and sustainable growth to happen, for the highest good of all.

The abundance of collective and personal wealth are key ingredients for a prosperous present and future for all. Matchmakers will act as bridges and connectors to turn personal wealth into collective abundance.

the IMPACT projects

That will shape the world for the better

ThreeFold Network is building a green and responsible peer-to-peer Internet that is available everywhere and owned by everyone – without borders nor geographical discrimination, empowers people to be digitally independent, and provides equal chances to learn, partake and succeed.

Kairospace represents a new concept of an Innovation Cooperative, aggregating the R&D and the core discoveries and technology being developed by the Kairospace networks, partners, associates and the research and development teams. There are a myriad of applications for what has been discovered and developed thus far, but even greater potential to be realized by aggregating and amortizing this R&D in a cross-pollinated way. Kairospace’s partners are backed up by a powerful Kairospace Advisory Board, bringing together a selection of impressive business, financial, humanitarian and scientific leaders.
